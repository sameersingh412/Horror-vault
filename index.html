The Whispering Room

In the old manor on Hollowridge Hill, there’s a room no one enters after sunset. The villagers call it The Whispering Room.

Eliza, a curious AI researcher, bought the estate to escape the noise of the city. She believed ghosts were just echoes of faulty perception—until the whispers started addressing her by name.

Every night at 2:13 AM, the voices returned—faint murmurs behind the walls. One night, she traced the sound to an old mirror nailed shut behind faded wallpaper.

She pried it free. The mirror showed her reflection—only it smiled when she didn’t.

When Eliza blinked, her reflection didn’t. It stepped forward and whispered, “I’ve waited centuries… to be real again.”

Now Eliza never leaves the room. But every few nights, the mirror whispers someone else’s name…

Maybe yours.
